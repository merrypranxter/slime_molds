# Mood Tags for Slime Mold Visual Systems

Aesthetic vibes, emotional registers, and art direction language. Each tag includes visual logic, species matches, and prompt application guidance.

---

## **ORGANIC HORROR**

Tags for work that leans into the unsettling, wrong, and flesh-adjacent.

### `grotesque`
- **Visual logic:** Excess, deformity, the obscene body — too much, too wet, too alive
- **Species match:** *Fuligo septica*, *Lycogala epidendrum*, *Tubifera ferruginosa*
- **Prompt application:** "grotesque maximalism, organic excess, wrong scale, flesh-adjacent color"

### `abject`
- **Visual logic:** The body's interior made exterior — things that should stay inside
- **Species match:** *Fuligo septica* (aethalium burst), plasmodium in flow
- **Prompt application:** "abject beauty, visceral horror, interior made exterior"

### `putrid`
- **Visual logic:** Decay aesthetics — the beautiful collapse of organic matter
- **Species match:** Sclerotium stage, collapsed aethalia, dead sporangia
- **Prompt application:** "putrid elegance, rot-slime coating, beautiful decomposition"

### `parasitic`
- **Visual logic:** Something growing on another thing, the host/parasite dynamic
- **Species match:** Any species on living bark, slime on other fungi
- **Prompt application:** "parasitic attachment, host invasion, colonization aesthetic"

### `wound-like`
- **Visual logic:** Surfaces that read as injury — blistered, burst, seeping
- **Species match:** *Fuligo septica* (aethalium cracking), ripe sporangia bursting
- **Prompt application:** "wound aesthetic, blistered surface, seeping matter, injury beauty"

## **DARK ELEGANCE**

Tags for work that is beautiful but threatens — death-adjacent, luxurious, cold.

### `gothic`
- **Visual logic:** Dark architecture, vertical structures, iridescence on black, Victorian death aesthetics
- **Species match:** *Stemonitis fusca*, *Lamproderma scintillans*, *Comatricha nigra*
- **Prompt application:** "gothic elegance, dark iridescence, pin-forest cathedral, Victorian biology"

### `memento mori`
- **Visual logic:** Death made beautiful, the decorative skull — dried structures, ghost architectures
- **Species match:** Dried sporangia, sclerotia, ghost stalk remains
- **Prompt application:** "memento mori aesthetic, death ornament, beautiful decay, still-life with mortality"

### `funereal`
- **Visual logic:** Black and grey with extreme care — solemn, formal, precise death
- **Species match:** *Stemonitis* cluster, *Comatricha*, dark Stemonitidales
- **Prompt application:** "funereal stillness, formal darkness, gothic ceremony, mourning aesthetic"

### `obsidian`
- **Visual logic:** Mirror-black surfaces, volcanic glass, hard and reflective darkness
- **Species match:** *Stemonitis* stipes, *Comatricha nigra* goblets
- **Prompt application:** "obsidian surface, volcanic black, mirror-dark reflective, hard darkness"

## **OTHERWORLDLY / ALIEN**

Tags for work that reads as non-human, non-terrestrial, or cognitively strange.

### `xenobiotic`
- **Visual logic:** Life that doesn't belong — wrong planet, wrong scale, wrong logic
- **Species match:** *Physarum polycephalum* network, *Ceratiomyxa fruticulosa* pillars
- **Prompt application:** "xenobiotic organism, alien biology, non-terrestrial life form, wrong planet"

### `distributed intelligence`
- **Visual logic:** A system that thinks without a brain — network logic, routing, optimization
- **Species match:** *Physarum polycephalum* maze-solving plasmodium
- **Prompt application:** "distributed cognition, non-neural computation, living algorithm, network intelligence"

### `bioluminescent`
- **Visual logic:** Light generated from within — deep sea logic applied to forest floor
- **Species match:** Any species reimagined with internal glow (interpretive, not literal)
- **Prompt application:** "bioluminescent interpretation, subsurface glow, deep-sea analog, living light"

### `cosmic horror`
- **Visual logic:** Scale wrongness — too large, incomprehensible, indifferent biology
- **Species match:** *Fuligo septica* at city scale, *Physarum* as continent-covering network
- **Prompt application:** "cosmic horror biology, incomprehensible scale, indifferent organism, Lovecraftian slime"

### `hive mind`
- **Visual logic:** Many individuals as one — loss of individual in collective
- **Species match:** *Dictyostelium discoideum* aggregation, *Physarum* fusing plasmodia
- **Prompt application:** "hive mind aesthetic, collective consciousness, individual dissolved, social organism"

## **FESTIVE / BAROQUE**

Tags for work that is lush, excessive, celebratory, and ornate.

### `baroque`
- **Visual logic:** Maximum ornamentation — no surface left undecorated
- **Species match:** *Arcyria denudata* capillitium, *Trichia varia* candelabra clusters
- **Prompt application:** "baroque excess, total ornamentation, festive maximalism, no empty space"

### `carnivalesque`
- **Visual logic:** Festivity at wrong scale — celebration hidden in forest rot
- **Species match:** *Arcyria* species, *Trichia*, any vivid Trichiales
- **Prompt application:** "carnivalesque micro, hidden celebration, festive at microscale, coral reef party"

### `jeweled`
- **Visual logic:** Precious materiality — gem-quality surfaces, light refraction
- **Species match:** *Cribraria argillacea* bead towers, *Lamproderma* iridescent, spore masses
- **Prompt application:** "jeweled surface, gemstone materiality, precious at microscale, mineral biology"

### `rococo`
- **Visual logic:** Delicate excess — light, lacy, impossibly detailed ornamentation
- **Species match:** *Arcyria cinerea* lace, *Cribraria cancellata* cages, capillitium networks
- **Prompt application:** "rococo biology, delicate excess, lace architecture, impossible fragility"

---

## **PURE / SACRED**

Tags for work that feels clean, transcendent, or spiritually toned.

### `pristine`
- **Visual logic:** Absolute cleanliness — white-on-white, unmarked surfaces
- **Species match:** *Ceratiomyxa fruticulosa*, *Diderma hemisphaericum*, *Didymium* species
- **Prompt application:** "pristine white, unmarked surface, clinical perfection, pure form"

### `crystalline`
- **Visual logic:** Geometric precision grown organically — crystal logic in living form
- **Species match:** *Ceratiomyxa fruticulosa* pillar arrays, *Diderma* frost hemispheres
- **Prompt application:** "crystalline growth, geometric precision, mineral logic in biology, crystal city"

### `sacred geometry`
- **Visual logic:** Mathematical perfection visible in organic form
- **Species match:** *Physarum polycephalum* routing networks, sorocarp arrays, spore patterns
- **Prompt application:** "sacred geometry, mathematical biology, divine proportion in slime, Fibonacci organism"

### `ethereal`
- **Visual logic:** So delicate it barely exists — translucency, near-invisibility
- **Species match:** *Ceratiomyxa* backlit, *Arcyria cinerea* grey lace, eggshell-stage sporangia
- **Prompt application:** "ethereal translucency, barely-there structure, ghost biology, dissolving form"

## **SCIENTIFIC / SYSTEMATIC**

Tags for work with a research or documentary aesthetic.

### `naturalist`
- **Visual logic:** Victorian specimen illustration — precise, labeled, objective beauty
- **Species match:** Any species in field documentation context
- **Prompt application:** "naturalist illustration, specimen plate, field guide aesthetic, biological accuracy"

### `clinical`
- **Visual logic:** Lab aesthetic — petri dish, agar plate, sterile observation
- **Species match:** *Dictyostelium discoideum* (actual lab organism), *Physarum* on agar
- **Prompt application:** "clinical observation, lab specimen, petri dish aesthetic, sterile precision"

### `diagrammatic`
- **Visual logic:** Exploded view, cross-section, labeled structure
- **Species match:** Any species in lifecycle diagram context
- **Prompt application:** "diagrammatic cross-section, exploded view, biological annotation, systematic beauty"

---

## **SOCIAL / CONCEPTUAL**

Tags for work exploring the behavioral and philosophical aspects.

### `collective`
- **Visual logic:** Many-as-one — the social contract made visible
- **Species match:** *Dictyostelium discoideum*, *Physarum polycephalum* fusing networks
- **Prompt application:** "collective behavior, social organism, democratic biology, swarm logic"

### `sacrifice`
- **Visual logic:** Structural death enabling reproduction — stalk cells dying for spore cells
- **Species match:** *Dictyostelium discoideum* sorocarp formation
- **Prompt application:** "sacrifice architecture, structural martyrdom, cellular altruism, death as foundation"

### `emergence`
- **Visual logic:** Complex behavior from simple rules — pattern from noise
- **Species match:** cAMP spiral waves, aggregation streams, vein network optimization
- **Prompt application:** "emergent complexity, pattern from chaos, simple rules complex behavior, biological emergence"
