# Texture Terms for Slime Mold Visual Description

Concrete sensory language for building prompts and describing surface qualities. Organized by tactile/visual category.

---

## **WET / MUCUS / GEL**

- **mucus-slick** — Wet, slimy surface with light reflection
- **custard-thick** — Dense gel consistency, opaque
- **vascular gel** — Translucent with visible internal structure
- **latex foam** — Wet foam with surface tension, bubble-like
- **translucent amber** — Honey-like gel, light passes through
- **edge-glow gel** — Backlit edges, subsurface scattering
- **wet-sheen** — Moisture on surface, reflective highlights
- **glycerin-thick** — Viscous, slow-flowing gel
- **slime-trail** — Residue path, glistening
- **mucoid drip** — Hanging droplets, viscosity visible

---

## **DRY / POWDER / DUST**

- **powder-dry** — Fine dust coating, matte finish
- **spore ash** — Loose powder, easily dispersed
- **velvety spores** — Soft powder texture, touchable feel
- **talc-fine** — Extremely fine powder, airborne
- **puffball dust** — Cloud-release texture
- **chalk-dry** — Dry matte, slight roughness
- **flour-soft** — Fine powder, light and airy
- **mica-shimmer** — Powder with iridescent particles
- **ash-residue** — Post-burn texture, grey powder
- **cocoa-powder** — Brown/rust fine powder

---

## **CRUSTY / CALCIFIED / HARDENED**

- **chalky** — White/pale crust, matte, porous
- **calcified** — Lime-encrusted, mineral deposit feel
- **pearl-dusted** — Iridescent white coating
- **crackled rind** — Dried surface with fissures
- **blistered** — Raised bubbles in crust
- **waxy crust** — Hardened with slight sheen
- **papery shell** — Thin dried layer, fragile
- **ceramic glaze** — Glossy hardened surface
- **frost-crusted** — Ice-like white coating
- **eggshell-brittle** — Thin, breakable crust

---

## **METALLIC / IRIDESCENT / REFLECTIVE**

- **metallic wet** — Liquid metal appearance
- **oil-slick iridescence** — Rainbow sheen on dark surface
- **chrome-sheen** — Mirror-like reflective
- **gunmetal-dark** — Dark grey metallic, matte
- **bronze-oxidized** — Aged metal patina
- **copper-verdigris** — Green-blue oxidation
- **silver-frosted** — Pale metallic with frost
- **mercury-beaded** — Liquid metal droplets
- **bismuth-crystal** — Geometric iridescent steps
- **steel-brushed** — Directional metallic texture

---

- **lather-thick** — Dense foam, wet
- **bath-foam** — Light wet bubbles
- **bread-sponge** — Porous internal structure
