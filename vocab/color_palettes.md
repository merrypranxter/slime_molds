# Color Palettes for Slime Mold Visual Systems

Specific hue and material descriptions for prompt engineering and species documentation. Organized by taxonomic group, lifecycle stage, and mood archetype.

## **PHYSARALES PALETTE**

Primary genus: *Fuligo*, *Physarum*, *Badhamia*, *Diderma*, *Didymium*

- `ochre gold` — #C9A84C — aged yellow, drying foam
- `cream tan` — #D4B896 — fully dried aethalium
- `chalky white` — #EDE8DF — lime crust surface
- `rust brown` — #8B4513 — substrate showing through cracks
- `carbon black` — #1A1A1A — deep substrate shadows

### Electric Network Group (*Physarum polycephalum*)
- `canary electric` — #FFE500 — fresh plasmodium, neon yellow
- `amber translucent` — #FFBF00 — gel body in light
- `gold dark` — #B8860B — drying/older plasmodium
- `sporangia brown` — #4A3728 — dried fruiting bodies
- `substrate grey` — #8A8A8A — agar/bark background

### Frost Chalk Group (*Diderma*, *Badhamia*)
- `chalky white` — #F5F0E8 — lime-encrusted peridium
- `pearl iridescent` — #E8E4F0 — slight lavender cast
- `ash grey` — #B0ADA8 — dried chalky mass
- `warm cream` — #F2EAD3 — substrate substrate tone

## **LIFECYCLE STAGE PALETTES**

### Plasmodium Stage
- Active (fresh): canary electric, translucent amber, edge-glow gel
- Stressed: amber deepening to gold, vein edges darkening
- Sclerotium: chocolate brown, cracked rind, waxy grey

### Spore Release Stage
- Spore mass colors: black, purple-brown, yellow, pale cream, rust
- Atmospheric: spore dust as fine cocoa powder, talc, ash
- Post-release: bleached grey-white skeleton

### Aggregation Stage (Dictyostelids)
- Cell body: translucent cream to pale tan
- Stream texture: dark cream lines on pale substrate
- Culmination: pure white rising structure

## **Prompt Template Format**

```
[species] [lifecycle stage], [color 1] [color descriptor], [color 2] [material], 
on [substrate color] substrate, [lighting], [render mode]
```

**Examples:**
- "*Physarum polycephalum* plasmodium, canary electric gel with translucent amber veins, on ash grey bark, backlit, macro photography"
- "*Arcyria denudata* fruiting body, coral red capillitium with salmon fading tips, on dark wood brown substrate, overcast diffuse light, SEM aesthetic"
- "*Lamproderma scintillans* sporangia cluster, violet iridescent peridium on dark sienna stalks, midnight blue shadow, macro photo, gothic elegance"
